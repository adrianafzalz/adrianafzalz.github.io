Submarine Monument

TLontong Balap is a culinary specialty of Surabaya, East Java, consisting of pieces of lontong, boiled bean sprouts, fried tofu, and lentho (peanut patties), topped with fresh sauce, and accompanied by petis chili sauce, sweet soy sauce, fried onions, and shrimp crackers. It is called “balap” because the vendors used to carry their wares quickly, giving the impression of racing. This dish is often paired with clam satay and coconut ice drink.

Rujak Cingur

Rujak cingur usually consists of slices of several types of fruit, such as cucumber, krai (a type of cucumber typical of East Java), jicama, young mango, pineapple, kedondong, and is added with lontong, tofu, tempeh, bendoyo, and cingur, as well as vegetables such as bean sprouts, kangkung, and long beans. All of these ingredients are mixed with a sauce or seasoning made from processed shrimp paste, boiled water to thin it slightly, sugar/brown sugar, chili, fried peanuts, fried onions, salt, and thin slices of young green bananas (pisang klutuk). All the sauces/seasonings are mixed by grinding, which is why rujak cingur is also often called rujak uleg.

Kalimas Boat Tour

Kalimas Boat Tour is one of Surabaya's leading tourist destinations. The sunset atmosphere and the sparkling lights of the magnificent buildings look very beautiful when enjoyed from aboard this tour boat. Kalimas Boat Tour operates every day from 3:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m. There are two departure piers, namely Taman Prestasi Pier and Monumen Kapal Selam Pier.
