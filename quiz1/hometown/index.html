The Lovely Surabaya

Surabaya is the capital of East Java Province, the center of regional government, politics, trade, industry, education, and culture. The city is famous as the City of Heroes because of its significant history in the struggle of Arek-Arek Suroboyo (the youth of Surabaya) to defend Indonesia's independence from colonial attacks. Surabaya is located in the center of Indonesia and just south of Asia, making it an important hub for trade in Southeast Asia. In addition, the city is one of the most important cities supporting Indonesia's economy. This is evidenced by Tanjung Perak Port, which is the second largest port in Indonesia after Jakarta. Tanjung Perak Port is also a trading port.
